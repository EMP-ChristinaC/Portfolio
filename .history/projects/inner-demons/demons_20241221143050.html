How To Play

Players can choose a character they want to fight as.

They can select a stage they want to fight in

They will start fighting with mechanics such as;
Punch; Walk; Jump; Crouch; and Block.

Players win by lowering their oppenents HP.

First person to K.O the other wins the game.
