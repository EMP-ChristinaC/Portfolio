Potato Shuffle

How To Play

Each player will take a arrow key.

Players will take turns pressing the key until a mini-game shows up.

The person that started the game has to hold their key.

When a game shows, the player has a limited amount of time to complete it.

Use the space bar to time your action or mash them.

When completed, it goes back to the players pressing the key one at a time.

Fail to complete the game or explode is out.

This keeps going until the last person stands.
