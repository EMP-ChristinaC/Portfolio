About:

The player wakes up in a cold, unfamiliar room. As they gather themselves and move out, a creature appears near the locker. Terrified, they wait until it leaves the room. Once calm, they emerge to search for clues about what happened.

How To Play

Fight against your friends to be the first to obtain the Ender Dragon Egg.

Trade with Villagers to obtain emeralds or resources.

Craft tools to help insure your victory by using their abilities.

Use your emeralds to gain the Ender Dragon Egg.
